Our Lamps

Romilda Italia pays great attention to the lighting. Light is the key ingredient to enhance and warm any room. Design, material quality and originality make the lamps special : the range is characterized by rigor and simplicity in the design of modern lamps, and by forms of memory to better combine with enveloping lines and romantic sofas. In the collection there are also more scenic lamps, in order to make more characteristic the Romilda Italia total living proposal. Discover the entire Romilda Italia collection in your nearest store. Our staff is at your disposal to find the best solution for every style, comfort and space need.
