Our Armchairs

The armchairs are the exceptional elements for an environment. For this reason, the Romilda Italia Group pays great attention to the production of these design elements. Shape and color have to be in perfect harmony. This idea inspires each model: from the creation of the draft to the selection of leathers and fabrics. Sartorial stitching and wooden features underline the Italian experience and craftsmanship with which are produced the armchairs. In addition, the high quality is certified by numerous checks along the entire production chain. Discover the entire Romilda Italia collection in your nearest store. Our staff is at your disposal to find the best solution for every style, comfort and space need.
