Our Rugs

The Romilda Italia Group found the best rugs in the world in Turkey and India. Surprisingly, in the East it discovered the concept of beauty in the dense and smooth weave of fine materials such as wool and viscose, recognizing in those masterpieces of fabric, attention to products. This care captures the senses with colors, textures, patterns and unique tactile sensations. All Romilda Italia handcrafted rugs are hypoallergenic and have the “Care and Fair“ warranty against illegal child labor. Discover the entire Romilda Italia collection in your nearest store. Our staff is at your disposal to find the best solution for every style, comfort and space need.
