Arioso

Comfortable both as a sofa and a bed, this modern sofa bed boasts large, accomodating seats. Features include an extraordinary backrest, which becomes both sofa headrest and headboard, once extended. The "Ready bed" mechanism ensures ease of operation, as with a single movement, you can go from sofa to bed and vice versa. Arioso is equipped with pillows which are stored in a special hidden compartment, making it a versatile storage-saver.

Options:
- Contrast welt
- Headrest

Comfort:
- The lumbar cushions are 100% goose feather for maximum luxurious comfort
- Seat cushion includes a layer of memory foam; a pressure-sensitive soft polyurethane, that recovers its form completely so that it always looks great while providing ultimate comfort 
- Mattress available in either Spring, Memory Foam or Greenplus(R), which is eco-friendly, hypoallergenic and antibacterial.
